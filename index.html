Body [Root]

The body is the root of design. Just a few overriding: font-size and font-family

The official font is 'Open Sans' from Google Fonts

Sample CSS:
    <link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Montserrat:400,400i,500,500i,600,600i,700,800|Open+Sans:400,400i,600,600i" rel="stylesheet">

Sample CSS:
    /*Override*/
    body {
        font-family: 'Open Sans';
        font-size: 16px;
    }
